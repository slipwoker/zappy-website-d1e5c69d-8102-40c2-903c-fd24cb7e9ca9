Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GRVMZ /KLōTHING/ BRAND. From shipping regions to care guidance and sustainability, we've got you covered.

What regions do you ship to?

We currently ship worldwide. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days depending on your location. Express options are available at checkout for select regions. All orders are tracked and insured.

How should I care for my garments?

To preserve the quality of your GRVMZ pieces, we recommend machine washing cold with like colors, using mild detergent, and hanging to dry. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners. Specific care labels are sewn into every garment.

What is your sustainability approach?

Sustainability is core to our production. We use organic cotton, recycled polyester, and low-impact dyes. Our packaging is 100% compostable, and we partner with factories that meet strict environmental and ethical standards.

How long do support responses take?

Our team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent inquiries regarding orders, please include your order number in the subject line. We are available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

Do you accept collaboration inquiries?

Absolutely. We are always open to collaborating with artists, designers, and creators who align with our brand ethos. Please reach out via our contact form with your portfolio and proposal, and our creative team will review it.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window for unworn, unwashed items with tags still attached. Returns are free for store credit. Refunds to the original payment method are subject to a small restocking fee. Final sale items are not eligible for return.
